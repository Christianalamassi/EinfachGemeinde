EinfachGemeinde

A simple church on every block of Berlin – a group of people committed to (1) Christ, (2) Christ in one another, and (3) loving Christ by loving one another and our neighbors.

We see these three commitments lived out in regularly meeting together as a community being devoted to the kernel of a Christ oriented faith community as summarized in the essence of the gathered believers kernel is Acts 2.42-47

42 They devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer. 43 Everyone was filled with awe at the many wonders and signs performed by the apostles. 44 All the believers were together and had everything in common. 45 They sold property and possessions to give to anyone who had need. 46 Every day they continued to meet together in the temple courts. They broke bread in their homes and ate together with glad and sincere hearts, 47 praising God and enjoying the favor of all the people. And the Lord added to their number daily those who were being saved.

We will actively pray for the Lord to raise up a simple church on every block of Berlin that would be described as a group of people committed to the good and peace of the city block through regularly sharing the love of God through good deeds and worshipping God in Christ together.

VISION

“On every block in Berlin, a simple church community is emerging—one that promotes the peace and well‑being of the block through regular acts of service (sharing God’s love) and celebrating together.”
